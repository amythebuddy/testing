About Me!

One of my childhood favorite memory is when I went to Vung Tau with my mom, and her friends. In case you don't know Vung Tau, it is a province that has beautiful beaches and delicious seafoods. I still rememeber the night when we went to the seafood market at night. We bought some crabs, snails, shrimps, and etc. All of it was so good that I still rememeber the smell, and the taste vividly.
